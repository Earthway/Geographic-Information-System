## Featured Projects

### Afforestation Suitability Mapping Using PyQGIS

A GIS-based project that identifies suitable areas for afforestation using slope analysis, land cover classification, rainfall suitability, and environmental factors. The project demonstrates the application of spatial analysis and geospatial automation for restoration planning.

**Status:** Ongoing

### Regeneration Kenya ARR Carbon Project

Development of an Afforestation, Reforestation, and Revegetation (ARR) carbon project covering 500 hectares across Baringo, Nakuru, and Elgeyo Marakwet Counties. The project aims to enhance carbon sequestration, biodiversity conservation, landscape restoration, and community livelihoods.

### Forest Carbon Stock Assessment

Application of forest inventory techniques and carbon accounting methodologies to estimate carbon sequestration potential and support climate mitigation initiatives.

### Climate and Environmental Mapping

Development of thematic maps and spatial analyses for environmental monitoring, conservation planning, climate resilience, and sustainable land-use management.

### Conservation and Restoration Planning

Preparation of concept notes and project proposals focusing on ecosystem restoration, community-based conservation, agroforestry, and climate resilience initiatives.
